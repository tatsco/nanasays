# Sister Mary Said
* [Live website](https://www.sistermarysaid.com/)
======
**Sister Mary Said** is a website influenced by the popular and well known fortune telling 'Magic 8 Ball' in which users ask the ball questions, shake and they instantly receive one of a variety of answers like "Yes definitely", "Cannot predict now" or "Outlook not so good".
In this case, Sister Mary (my blunt and wise grandmother) is the fortune teller.
